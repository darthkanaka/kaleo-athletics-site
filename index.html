Questions parents and clients actually ask

Where do sessions happen?

Training locations are set per client: a park, a field, a garage gym, wherever fits the plan. Massage is fully mobile: Kaleo brings the table, linens, and everything else to your home, anywhere from Mililani to Pearl City.

Is strength training safe for a middle schooler?

Done right, yes: the research is clear that supervised, age-appropriate strength work is safe and protective for young athletes. Done right means movement quality before load, total weekly load managed around team practice, and a coach who knows when the answer is rest. That's the job.

Do you guarantee scholarships or recruiting exposure?

No, and nobody honest can. What Kaleo controls is whether your athlete shows up prepared: stronger, faster, more durable, with habits that survive the jump. Preparation, never placement.

What if I just want a relaxing massage, not sports work?

Then that's what you get. You set the pressure, and the session serves you, not a script. The coaching background just means he understands what your body does all day, whether that's squats or a desk.

Do you offer lomilomi?

No. Lomilomi is its own practice with its own lineage, and Kaleo doesn't claim it. He practices sports and therapeutic massage as a licensed massage therapist.
